Welcome to my Justin Townes Earle Tribute Page!

Here you will learn more about JTE and why I like him.


Justin Townes Earle was a county/folk musical artist. He was born in 1982 to musician Steve Earle and Carol Ann Hunter Earle. In his career, Earle played at the Grand Ole Opry, the Winnipeg Folk Festival and the Nelsonville Music Festival. He also recieved multiple Americana Music Awards. He died in August of 2020 at the age of 38 from an accidental drug overdose. He left behind a wife and a young daughter.

You can learn more on his Wikipedia page and his website.
