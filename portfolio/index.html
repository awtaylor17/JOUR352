Alexander Taylor - Reporter

Bio

Alexander Taylor is a junior at the University of Maryland, working towards his B.A. in Journalism with an economics concentration. He currently serves as the News Programming Director at WMUC, UMD's student-run radio station. He works on two news shows at WMUC in music and sports, and manages a team of UMD reporters covering breaking news. Taylor has previously worked for The Baltimore Banner, Capital News Service, the South Baltimore Peninsula Post, and The Baltimore Witness. He has experience in HTML, Canva, Premiere Pro, Adobe Audition, Photoshop, and After Effects. When not covering breaking news, Taylor participates in UMD's martial arts club.
