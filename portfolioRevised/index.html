Alexander Taylor - Reporter

Bio

Alexander Taylor is a junior at the University of Maryland, College Park, where he studies Journalism. He currently serves as the News Programming Director at WMUC, UMD's student-run radio station. Taylor has previously worked for The Baltimore Banner, Capital News Service, the South Baltimore Peninsula Post and Baltimore Witness. He is skilled in Canva, Premiere Pro, Adobe Audition, Photoshop and After Effects. When not covering breaking news, Taylor helps run UMD's martial arts club.
